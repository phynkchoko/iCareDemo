Our Valued Services

Services We Offer

Nanny

our nannies provide child care within the family setting. They will care for the children while both parents work or engage in other important activities.

She generally assume responsibility over the child in creating daily schedules and engage in activities to ensure healthy mental, physical, and emotional wellbeing in the children they care for. Most nannies will be tasked with responsibilities like preparing meals, helping with household work, laundry, washing dishes, driving the children to and from activities, pickup at school, assisting with homework, etc.A nanny could be Full-Time Live-In Nanny, Full-Time Live-Out Nanny or even a Part-Time Nanny.

Readmore...

Babysitter

Our babysitters will care temporarily for children on behalf of the parents or guardians. They generally take care of children of all ages who are in need of supervision while the parents are away.

Most babysitting jobs are considered part-time jobs and are generally scheduled regularly (e.g., every day from 3-6 p.m. or every Saturday night) or for special occasions (e.g., New Year's Eve). Above all, a babysitter is responsible for the safety and well-being of your children while they're in her care.

Readmore...

House Help

This is a unique type of nanny, typically a young girl or woman and sometimes a young man, employed to help with housework or child care.

If He/she is a student, sometimes the arrangement could be in exchange for accomodation, food and school fees. In other cases, service seekers pay a monthly stipend (little salary).

This is a very affordable option, but you would be responsible for providing food and other necessities to make the house help comfortable.

Elderly Caregiver

Our Elderly care emphasizes giving the best social and personal treatments to our aged citizens who need some assistance with daily activities, health care, and who desire to age with dignity.

It encompasses everything from assisted living, providing companionship and nursing care to ageing adults.

Our Elderly care could be "Live in" or "Live out" caregivers.

Transportation and Errand Care

Our Transportation and Errand care service provides transportation to patients who require transportation to and from a medical facility for treatment or physical exams.

They also provide accompaniment on errands such as grocery shopping, medical appointments, dry cleaning, prescription pickup and other activities.

Personal Assistant

Our personal assistants will assist clients with their daily business or personal tasks. Do you run a business? manage the needs of a large family? or have too much to do in a day, you probably need a personal assistant.

Our Personal assistant duties vary based on individual needs, and can be customized to fit your lifestyle, business, or family.
